This was our group's first attempt at an insect biodiversity survey. The rain had just lifted, and the island was lush with spring vitality. We had an unexpected companion: a local dog that followed us through the fields for the whole day! But the ground was a muddy mess, and to make matters worse, our supervisor managed to run the three-wheel car into a roadside ditch. Memorable, to say the least.
